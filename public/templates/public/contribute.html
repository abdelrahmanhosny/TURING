Why Contributing to TURING?..

  • Thanks to AlgoRun, it requires no code change to package your algorithm.
  • The underlying Docker technology used ensures that your algorithm will always work in the future;
    no broken installation links or outdated libraries.
  • TURING gives your algorithms visibility and ease of use for your audience.
  • All AlgoRun-based algorithms can be used to construct data analysis workflows using AlgoPiper in a few clicks
  • Build up your public profile and get more citation on your research.
